Unwavering Will

A mind ablaze, a spirit bright,
A quest for knowledge, a noble sight.
Through trials and errors, doubts and fears,
A steadfast heart, that perseveres.

The weight of books, a heavy load,
Yet shoulders strong, along the road.
Late nights of study, a weary sigh,
But dawn breaks forth, a promise nigh.

With every challenge, a lesson learned,
A resilience forged, a spirit burned.
Each setback, a chance to rise,
With renewed vigor, to touch the skies.

For in the depths of academic strife,
Resides a power, a force of life.
A mind that bends, but never breaks,
A scholar's journey, for goodness' sake.
